MEMORY REBOOT

In the first part of the movie, we are introduced to the dystopian world of the year 2049, where human society coexists with replicants, androids designed to serve humans. The protagonist, K, is a blade runner tasked with retiring rogue replicants. During a routine mission, he uncovers a long-hidden secret that could change the course of history: the possibility that a replicant could give birth to a child.
