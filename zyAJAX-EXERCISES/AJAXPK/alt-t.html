The it's important to note that when inflating a tire above three bars, the pump requires more time for each additional bar and the temperature can increase. This inevitably leads to a faster depletion of your battery capacity.

The Coolado tPump XL pump is equipped with a built-in sensor that detects when the temperature exceeds an acceptable level, causing it to automatically shut off for safety.

We want to warn you that if you are inflating tires with a pressure between 3-5.5 bars, the pump may shut off and need to cool down before you can continuje inflating.
